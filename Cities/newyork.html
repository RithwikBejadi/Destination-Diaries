The feeling of history here is amazing. It's one thing to see this place on the internet and TV, but to be here stood in front of the pyramids is surreal. Best of the Months to visit:

The best time to visit the Giza Pyramids is from October to February, when the weather is mild and comfortable, with average temperatures ranging from 15°C to 20°C (59°F to 68°F). This is also the peak tourist season, but it's worth it for the pleasant weather and longer days.
